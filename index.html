How it works

Three steps. That's it.

No complex setup, no integrations, no learning curve.

1

Upload your CSV

Drop in a spreadsheet with email addresses, subjects, and bodies. Extra columns like {{name}} or {{company}} become merge tags automatically.

2

Preview every email

See exactly what each recipient will receive. Mailcue auto-detects your columns and fills in personalisation tags so nothing gets sent by mistake.

3

Schedule and send

Pick a time or spread sends across a window so they arrive naturally. Emails send on time, even if you close Chrome.
